Who is Ed Kainoki?

The fictional KAINOKI, Ed is the name that we offer as a focal point for the group that has built The Kainoki Treebank, comprised of:

  • BUTLER, Alastair
  • HAYASHI, Noritsugu
  • HIYAMA, Shota
  • HORN, Stephen Wright
  • ISHIKAWA, Sakura
  • ITO, Katsumasa
  • KATAKURA, Natsuha

  • KUBOTA, Ai
  • KURIBAYASHI, Takayuki
